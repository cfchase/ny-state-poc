There is not a notification or disclosure requirement under FERPA, however, there is a requirement to record access and disclosures (34 CFR ยง 99.32). In addition, the U.S. Department of Education considers notification a best practice. Furthermore, New York State Education Law Section 2-d includes notification requirements in the event of an unauthorized release of student data.
